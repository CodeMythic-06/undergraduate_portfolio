Biography

Recently graduated, already building.

I'm Kwame Mensah, a front-end web designer and developer based in Accra, Ghana. I recently completed my degree in Computer Science, where a general elective in web development turned into the thing I spent the rest of my degree chasing — every free assignment slot became an excuse to rebuild some part of my portfolio.

What draws me to the front end is the immediate feedback loop: change a value in a stylesheet, refresh the browser, and see the effect instantly. I like that discipline of small, deliberate adjustments — spacing, contrast, the width of a column — until an interface feels obvious rather than clever.

I build with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. That keeps sites fast, maintains clean markup for future maintainers, and forces me to understand every design decision.

Outside of client work, I mentor first-year students at my old department's coding club and spend an unreasonable amount of time redesigning websites I use daily, purely for practice.
